# Mafia Privacy Policy
This section is for transparency when publishing the app on the Google Play Store.

For an overview, The Mafia application:
- Does not collect, transmit, share or use any Personal Data.
- Does not contain advertisements or trackers.
- Has no restricted parts within the app.
- Is a social deduction group game targeted at ages 13+.
- Is Open Source Free Software with the code publicly available at https://www.github.com/3omargaad/Mafia
- Does not use any high-risk or sensitive permissions such as SMS/Call Log permissions.
- Uses stable versions of python libraries.
- Is not a News or Magazine app.
